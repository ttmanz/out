Privacy first

Your location, your rules

Find-Mee never tracks you without your knowledge. Location sharing is always opt-in, temporary, and in your hands.

✦

Check-in location is only visible to others for 3 hours, then automatically hidden

✦

Only shared with members within 300 metres of you

✦

Revoke location permission any time in your device settings

✦

Profile visibility settings — public, friends-only, or private
