## ENGLISH (en-GB)

### Short Description (80 chars max)
```
Go out, find people nearby and plan unforgettable nights out.
```

### Full Description (4000 chars max)
```
Find - Mee is the social app built for people who actually go out.

Whether you're heading to a bar, a rooftop, a club, or a beach party — Find-Mee connects you with real people who are out at the same venues, planning the same kinds of nights, and looking to meet.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

📍 At Venue — Check in at your venue and see who else is nearby. Connect with people within 300 metres. Your location is automatically hidden after you leave.

🏠 Home Feed — Follow friends, share moments from your night, post stories that disappear after 24 hours, and discover what's happening around you.

📅 Night Out Planner — Create or join a Night Out. Set the date, time, venue, and invite people. See who's in and plan the night together.

💬 Chat — Message friends and groups directly inside the app. Start conversations, share venues, plan your evening in real time.

🛒 The Market — Buy and sell with people in your social circle. From event tickets to pre-loved items — all within your trusted community.

🎉 Happenings — Discover local events, parties, and activities happening near you. Join a Happening or create your own.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Create your profile — add a photo, your interests, and the city you go out in.
2. Go out — check in At Venue to see who's nearby.
3. Connect — send friend requests, start chats, join Night Outs.
4. Plan — use the Night Out Planner to organise your perfect evening.

PRIVACY FIRST

Your location is only shared when you choose to check in. It's shown to a limited radius (300m) and automatically hidden after 3 hours. You control everything — what's visible, who can message you, and when you appear.

Find-Mee is built for Cyprus and Greece but open to anyone who loves going out.

Find - Mee. Go out. Connect. Meet people.
```
